What we believe

Ownership over access

Software should give you more control, not less. No gate-keeping. No tenant traps.

Simplicity as practice

A simple system is easier to host, debug, trust, change, and keep alive for years.

Complexity must justify itself

We would rather make a sharp tool than a vague platform. Every layer must earn its place.

Data that stays yours

Plain formats. Visible moving parts. Integration points that don't require surrender.

Security by shape

Security and privacy built into the tool's design — not pasted on as branding.

Preserved exits

Adopt our tools and become more independent, not less. Leave without losing anything.

Manifesto

We do not want software that only works as long as someone else keeps the gate open. We do not want products that create artificial complexity in order to justify themselves. We do not want systems that slowly turn their users into tenants.

We build software that stays close to its purpose.
